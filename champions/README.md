# 🏆 ORSTAC Trio of Champions (Elite Bots)

This directory contains the final selection of the best robots for DBot (Deriv), following a full technical audit of over 500 files. These 3 robots were chosen because they represent the three pillars of consistent trading operations: **Mathematical Confluence**, **Chart Analysis (Price Action)**, and **Conservative Risk Management**.

## 1. The Mathematical Brain: `safe profit pro.xml`
- **Why it's a champion:** It's the robot with the best confluence of technical indicators. It doesn't enter the market by "luck," but rather when the Moving Average (EMA), Relative Strength Index (RSI), and MACD all confirm the same direction.
- **Ideal for:** Medium-term operations where entry precision is more important than the quantity of trades.

## 2. The Chart Analyst: `Candlestick v2.xml`
- **Why it's a champion:** Unlike most bots that only look at isolated numbers, this robot has logic to identify real Price Action patterns (Hammers, Shooting Stars, Engulfing). It's the most "human-like" robot in the collection.
- **Ideal for:** Markets with clear volatility, where candle patterns repeat frequently.

## 3. The Bankroll Manager: `Rise-Fall - Consistent - Trends.xml`
- **Why it's a champion:** It's the master of survival. While other robots use aggressive Martingales that can blow an account in minutes, this one uses a very low recovery multiplier (1.071x), focusing on slow and steady growth.
- **Ideal for:** Long trading sessions ("set and forget") and for traders with smaller bankrolls who cannot accept large drawdowns.

---
**Security Note:** Even though these are the best, always test in a **DEMO** account for at least 24 hours before going to a Real account. Consistency comes from patience and testing.
