STRATEGY: CANDLESTICK V2
--------------------------
TYPE: Price Action / Pattern Recognition

OPERATIONAL LOGIC:
This is one of the smartest robots in the library. It analyzes the 4 main points of each candle (Open, Close, High, and Low) to identify formations such as:
- Hammer: Bullish reversal signal.
- Shooting Star: Bearish reversal signal.
- Bullish/Bearish Engulfing: Strong market pressure signals.

It only enters the trade when a high-probability pattern is formed on the previous candle.

RECOMMENDED SETTINGS:
- Candle Interval: 1 minute (60s)
- Focus: Identifying reversals at support and resistance.
- This robot requires patience, as it may take several minutes between trades.
