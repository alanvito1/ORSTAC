STRATEGY: RISE-FALL CONSISTENT
-------------------------------
TYPE: Risk Management and Capital Preservation

OPERATIONAL LOGIC:
This robot is designed to "win by exhaustion." Its entry strategy is simple (based on short-term trend), but its secret lies in its MANAGEMENT:
- It uses a Martingale multiplier of only 1.071x.
- This means that in case of a loss, it doesn't try to recover everything at once by doubling the bet.
- It recovers capital gradually over several wins, protecting your bankroll against long losing streaks (Red).

RECOMMENDED SETTINGS:
- Usage: Ideal for running for long periods (1h to 3h).
- Bankroll: Excellent for accounts starting from 50 USD.
- Safety: The safest robot for those prioritizing NOT blowing the account.
