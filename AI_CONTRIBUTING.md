# AI-Powered Trading Bot Development

At ORSTAC, we believe that the future of trading is algorithmic and AI-driven. We don't just allow AI; we **encourage** and **expect** our contributors to use it.

## The AI Advantage

Artificial Intelligence can process vast amounts of data, identify micro-patterns that humans miss, and optimize trading parameters with mathematical precision. 

### Why Use AI?
- **Pattern Recognition**: Use AI to identify the best entry and exit points.
- **Optimization**: Run simulations to find the perfect Martingale multiplier or Stop Loss.
- **Speed**: Develop and iterate on new strategies in minutes instead of weeks.

## Don't Get Left Behind

The trading world is evolving. Those who rely solely on manual logic or old-school methods will eventually be outpaced by those leveraging the power of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Machine Learning.

**To stay competitive in the trading race, you MUST integrate AI into your workflow.**

## Recommended Tools
- **ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini**: For writing and debugging XML block logic and strategies.
- **Python Scripts**: For backtesting the logic before converting it to Deriv XML blocks.
- **Custom ML Models**: For those who want to take it to the next level.

## How to Contribute AI-Generated Bots
When you use AI to help build a bot:
1. Mention the AI tool used in your Pull Request.
2. Share the prompt or logic flow used to generate the strategy.
3. Be transparent about the testing and validation you performed.

**Join the AI revolution. Build smarter, trade better.**
